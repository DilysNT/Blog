Porcelain crowns (also known as dental caps) are custom-made covers placed on top of damaged teeth...

1. When do you need a Dental Crown?

A dental crown may be needed in the following situations:

  • To protect a weak tooth from breaking or to hold together parts of a cracked tooth.
  • To restore an already broken tooth or a tooth that has been severely worn down.

2. Advantages of Porcelain Crowns

Porcelain or ceramic crowns provide the best natural color match than any other crown type and may be more suitable for people with metal allergies.
